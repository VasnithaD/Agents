1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
The purpose of the project is to develop a mobile application that allows customers to easily order groceries, select delivery time slots, and make online payments. This solution aims to enhance customer convenience and streamline the ordering process for FreshBasket's stores in Hyderabad.

2. PROJECT BACKGROUND
----------------------------------------
FreshBasket Grocery Chain aims to modernize its shopping experience by providing an app that allows customers to order groceries conveniently. Currently, customers face challenges in ordering and managing their grocery needs, and the new app seeks to address these pain points by offering a straightforward and user-friendly solution tailored to the needs of both customers and store managers.

3. SCOPE
----------------------------------------
In Scope  : Development of a customer-facing mobile app with a product catalog, cart functionality, slot-based delivery, online payment integration, user registration with OTP login, and a lightweight admin panel for store managers to manage orders and inventory.
Out Scope : The project will not include email login options, advanced features beyond inventory management, or any integration with third-party delivery services.

6. RISKS
----------------------------------------
  RISK-001: There is a risk that customers may face difficulties using the app if it is not user-friendly, especially since many are not tech-savvy.
  RISK-002: There is a risk of potential security breaches concerning the handling of online payment information.

7. ASSUMPTIONS
----------------------------------------
  ASSUMPTION-001: It is assumed that customers will prefer OTP login over email registration for accessing the app.
  ASSUMPTION-002: It is assumed that store managers will be able to effectively use the lightweight admin panel despite their limited technical skills.

8. CONSTRAINTS

  CONSTRAINT-001: The app will only be developed for the FreshBasket stores located in Hyderabad.
